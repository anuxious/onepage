About

Hello! I am Anoushka. I 'm 15 years old and I 'm in 11th grade studying STEM subjects. I 'm a Kathak Dancer and have been dancing since I was 3 and have a national level diploma in Kathak Dance.

I 'm also a singer and have been doing Indian Classical Music for more than have a decade now. I can also play the harmonium and keyboard along with a little bit of tabla.

Additionally, I 'm a Full Stack Developer who has a strong backbone in Front End Web Development. I 'm also a beginner at App Development and Ethical Hacking. I 've also been learning Artificial Intelligence for a few years now and I 'm really interested in pursuing it along with Computer Engineering in the near future.

This is not my portfolio which is something you can email me to get ! I 'm also a Graphic Designer who is worked in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. I also enjoy creating small hand drawn designs on Procreate and own a few POD businesses to sell these designs in forms of articles like apparel, accessories etc.

I 'd say I genuinely enjoy helping others a lot and am a feminist who advocates for Mental Health especially in Children and young adults. I 'm the founder of the Non Profit Organisations: Theraprize and Groceree and I also conduct various fundraiser events and campaigns on a very regular basis.

Collaborative Projects

Connect : Texting and Video Calling Android App

Lead Designer and co creator

Download this Android app created in collaboration with Fleure and Aalizah which works as a fully functional texting app with all the features you'd need.

We are yet to upload it on Google Play because we're still working on fixing bugs and glitches along with improving the current features and hence any feedback on the application will be much appreciated. If the link doesn't work on clicking, please send any of us an email and we will send you the apk file via mail.
If you recieve a "unsafe file "notification, please do not worry, it is absolutely safe and the notification only shows up because it hasn 't been published on Google Play yet (something I am working on currently doing).

You can perform the following functions:
➛ Dark and Light Mode
➛ Chat Wallpapers
➛ Audio Messages
➛ Unsending Messages
➛ Creating groupchats
➛ Inviting others
